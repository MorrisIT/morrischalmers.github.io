About me

My name is Jonathan Svantesson and I am born in Gothenburg, Sweden. I am 23 years old and currently a second-year student at University of Chalmers. One of my biggest interests in life has always been sports. Everything from surfing in Portugal to skiing in the French Alps, but especially football, which I have been playing for as long as I can remember. When I am not playing sports, I love to travel. I have been to a lot of places in my life, which has made me constantly strive for adventure, discovering new places and opportunities, meeting new people, and trying new things.

Other than studies and sports, I have had the opportunity to work with many different people over the years. For 5 years, over the summers and winters, I got to work at the amusement park Liseberg, where I was part of a restaurant staff team of up to 25 people. For the two last years I even got to take up the role as a shift manger. I then thought my time in restaurants was over, however, I worked one last time as a waiter in Slottsskogen, Gothenburg, before continuing my studies. At the start of my university years, I studied for one year at the University of Gothenburg, in the Information Systems Analysis program. However, I was eager to take on a more significant challenge. Therefore, I started a five-year Master of Science degree in engineering at the University of Chalmers. The field I am specializing in is Computer Science.

I hope I have given you an enriched picture of who I am. If you want to know more, feel free to contact me! Best regards Jonathan
